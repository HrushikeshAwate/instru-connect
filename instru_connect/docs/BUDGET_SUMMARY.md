## Cost Justification & ROI Analysis

### Benefits Realized

#### 1. Operational Efficiency
- **Administrative Time Savings**: 20-30 hours/month
  - Reducing manual attendance tracking
  - Eliminating paper-based processes
  - Automating notifications and reports
  - **Estimated Value**: ₹8,000-12,000/month

#### 2. Cost Reductions
- **Paper & Printing Costs**: ₹2,000-3,000/month reduction
- **Infrastructure Modernization**: Long-term cost reduction through digital systems
- **Reduced Manual Errors**: Better data accuracy = fewer corrections needed

#### 3. Revenue Impact
- **Improved Student Satisfaction**: Better technology = improved reputation
- **Recruitment Advantage**: Modern infrastructure attractive to students
- **Faculty Productivity**: More time for teaching, less on administration

### Return on Investment (ROI) Calculation

**Year 1 Investment**: ₹166,100
**Estimated Annual Benefits**: 
- Time savings (25 hours/month × ₹400/hour): ₹120,000
- Paper/printing savings: ₹30,000
- Operational efficiency: ₹50,000
- **Total Annual Benefit**: ₹200,000+

**Year 1 ROI**: (₹200,000 - ₹166,100) / ₹166,100 × 100 = **20.4% positive ROI**

**Break-even point**: Less than 1 year (approximately 10 months)

## Appendix: Vendor Pricing Details

### Firebase Pricing (as of March 2026)

**Free Tier Limits** (sufficient for small deployment):
- Realtime Database: 1GB storage, 100 connections
- Cloud Firestore: 1GB storage, 50K reads/day
- Cloud Storage: 5GB storage, 1GB download/day
- Authentication: 50K MAUs

**Paid Tier** (per the estimates above):
- Pay-as-you-go model
- Scale with usage
- No setup fees
- Cancel anytime

### App Store Fees

**Apple App Store**:
- Enrollment fee: $99/year (₹8,330)
- Commission: 15-30% (for paid apps; free apps no commission)

**Google Play Store**:
- Registration: ₹2,000 (one-time)
- Enrollment renewal: Free
- Commission: 15-30% (for paid apps; free apps no commission)
